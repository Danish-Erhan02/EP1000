Ultrasonic-Sensing Bin





What inspired my idea?

At the time of making this project, we live in a world ravasged by the coronavirus. This project aims to reduce the contamination of bacteria in the environment by allowing users to throw in their rubish without contacting the bin. And the rubbish will also be enclosed so as to prevent scavenger animals from flocking to the rubbish bin.
