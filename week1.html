An Intoduction to FabLab

As the name suggests, FabLab is a small scale workshop offering digital fabrication. A fab lab is typically equipped with an array of flexible computer-controlled tools that cover several different length scales and various materials, with the aim to make "almost anything".

FabLab Safety

Students are required to be dressed in proper attire in the FabLab.
Long hair must be tied up, adorments must be kept, covered shoes has to be worned. T-shirts must be short sleeved and must not be loose.

Always read and understand the info printed on the safety warning placards before using the machines.

Know the boundaries of the machine(ie; perimeter, drive train, points of operation)

Beware of:

☛ Entanglement Hazards
☛ Cutting Hazards
☛ Impact Hazazrds
☛ Shearing Hazards
☛ Crushing Hazards
☛ Draw-in Hazards
☛ Frinction and Abrasion Hazards
