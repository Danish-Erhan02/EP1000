Laptop Stand



First I chose the vetical sketch plane to sketch a single plane of the knight piece base.




Then, I used the revolve funtion to revolve the sketch 360 degrees forming the base.




Next, I choose a photo of a knight piece from the internet and save it in my computer. I used the insert Canvas function to insert the photo of the knight piece onto a sketch plane. After that, I traced the outline of knight piece in the canvas.




I then extruded the sketch of the knight piece and used the combine function to combine the base with the knight head.
